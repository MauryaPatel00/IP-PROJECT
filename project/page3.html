Khel Mahakumbh 2022 – Registration Online, Age Limit, Sports, Prizes

November 21, 2022 by Ritesh Salvi

Khel Mahakumbh 2022: On 12th March 2022, Indian Prime Minister Narendra Modi, Gujarat Chief Minister Shri Bhupendra Patel, and Gujarat Governor Acharya Devvrat opened the 11th edition of the ‘Khel Mahakumbh’ in Ahmedabad. Players along with the Divyang players from all around the state will compete against each other in a variety of games. Prizes of more than Rs.30 crores will be awarded among the players to encourage people to participate in the Khel Mahakumbh 2022. You may learn more about the Khel Mahakumbh by visiting their official website or by continuing to read our post.

Khel Mahakumbh Prizes

All those participants who will win in their respective games will be awarded as per the decided cash prizes. The top three winners will be awarded on the basis of their District/Taluka level. The District level participant who will get the 1st position will receive Rs. 1.5 Lakhs, the one with the 2nd position will get Rs. 1 lakhs and the 3rd one will get Rs. 75,000. Similarly, on the Taluka level, the player who will be in 1st place will get Rs. 75,000, the 2nd one will get Rs. 15,000 and the 3rd one will get Rs. 10,000 as the cash prize amount.

How to register for Khel Mahakumbh 2022?

With the help of this section, you will get familiar with the stages of how to register for Khel Mahakumbh 2022:

  • Visit the Khel Mahakumbh official website khelmahakumbh.gujarat.gov.in.
  • Tick on the ‘KhelMahakumbh-Login/Registration’ box given on the right side of the page.
  • You will be redirected to a new web page and various options will be visible on your screen.
  • If you are applying as a single player then tick on the ‘Individual Registration’ and the registration form will get open.
  • Start filling up the details asked inside the form and after completing them tick on the ‘Submit Form.
  • The message of successful submission will be visible on your screen.

The Divyang participants can download their Khel Mahakumbh registration form from the link available under the section of ‘Whats New’ on the main page.
